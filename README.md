# DirectApply Filter

DirectApply Filter helps job seekers keep search results focused on roles worth applying to directly. It reduces noise from staffing agencies, promoted listings, and unwanted sources so you can scan job boards faster and spend more time on real opportunities.

## Key Features

- Automatically hides third-party job sources, including aggregators, job boards, and staffing firms.
- Filters promoted LinkedIn job listings with a dedicated toggle.
- Supports custom blocklists for companies and job sources you want to avoid.
- Adds a one-click Block action to LinkedIn job cards.
- Includes a whitelist for employers that should always stay visible.
- Adds reason tags so hidden results clearly show why they were filtered.
- Adds optional placeholders so hidden results can be reviewed or restored.
- Offers a vanish mode for removing hidden results without placeholder rows.
- Tracks how many jobs were hidden in the current search.
- Keeps preferences on your device.

## Built For Job Search

DirectApply is designed for repeated, high-volume job searching. Use it to clean up noisy result pages, remove recurring low-value listings, and keep your attention on companies you actually want to evaluate.

## Platform Support

DirectApply currently supports LinkedIn Jobs.

LinkedIn support includes source filters, custom blocklists, whitelists, hidden-job counts, placeholders, and promoted-job hiding.

## Privacy

Your blocklists, whitelist, toggles, and hidden-job counts stay in Chrome storage on your device. DirectApply does not require an account, analytics, or a remote service.

## Installation

1. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions`.
2. Enable Developer mode.
3. Choose Load unpacked.
4. Select this folder.
5. Open a supported job board and use the DirectApply popup to manage filters.

## Controls

- **Enable filter**: Turn all filtering on or off.
- **Show placeholders**: Keep a visible marker where a job was hidden.
- **Vanish hidden jobs**: Remove hidden jobs without placeholder rows or Show buttons.
- **Use built-in source list**: Apply the included third-party source filter.
- **Hide promoted jobs**: Remove promoted LinkedIn listings.
- **Lists**: Manage custom blocklist, whitelist, and keyword filters.
- **Block**: Add a company or job source to your blocklist from a LinkedIn job card.
